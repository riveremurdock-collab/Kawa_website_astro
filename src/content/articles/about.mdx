This includes trip reports, product reviews, tutorials, and more. Use the inventory menu to explore the features of the website and to find the link to the shop. Kawa Designs is a product design brand created by River Murdock, based in Salt Lake City, Utah. After spending years making gear for his own adventures, River decided to share his passion for making equipment with others. He specializes in creating gear for hiking, backpacking, bikepacking, and everyday use. Each piece is designed to be durable, versatile, and environmentally friendly, using recycled and natural technical fabrics. All Kawa products are designed, tested, and made by hand in Salt Lake City, Utah." />
